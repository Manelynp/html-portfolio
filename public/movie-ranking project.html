Top Movies of All Time According to Manelyn

My Best 3 Movies.

Avatar (2009 Film)

This is my favorite science fiction film. I am amazed with their visual effects and immersive 3D technology.

Pirates of the Caribbean

An adventure Film Series that is really cool. I like the adventure of a witty and eccentric pirate in the Caribbean Captain Jack Sparrow.

Encanto (Disney Film)

This is a Disney animated musical film that I love to watch. I love their beautiful animation, catchy songs, and heartwarming themes of family and self-discovery.
